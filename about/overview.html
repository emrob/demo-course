About This Course

Welcome to Appsembler's Intro to Open edX course! This course is designed to get you up and running with Open edX as quickly as possible. There is a ton of cool stuff Open edX can do. Some of it is extremely simple, and some of it is a little more complicated. This course focues more on the simple stuff so you can quickly be on your way to creating great course content.

Appsembler are the Open edX enterprise experts. We specialize in hosting your Open edX site, and dealing with all the difficult technical details so you can focus on make awesome courses.

Requirements

The only requirement for this class is that you have a desire to learn more about Open edX and how to create great courses!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to buy a textbook?

Nope. This course uses no textbook. However, it will occassionally refer to the official Open edX documentation, which can be found from the Course Info page.

Will this course teach me how to host Open edX?

No. This course focuses primarily on course basic authoring and instructing techniques that are used in Open edX. If you are interested in hosting Open edX, check out our blog post on Why Hosting Open edX is so Complicated.
